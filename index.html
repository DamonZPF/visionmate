Privacy Policy

Welcome to our VisionMate App (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). The App is designed to assist visually impaired individuals by providing functionalities such as object recognition, navigation, and information retrieval. Before using the App, please read and understand this Privacy Policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

1.1 User-provided Information

The App may request you to provide personal information, including but not limited to your name, contact details, email address, etc., for user identity verification and to offer personalized services. You can choose whether to provide such information, but not providing it may affect the use of certain features.

1.2 Camera Images

To enable object recognition functionality, the App will use your device's camera to capture images. These images may contain some personal information, such as facial features. However, these images will only be used for object recognition and navigation purposes and will not be stored or used for any other purpose.

1.3 Device Information

The App may automatically collect and record information about your device, including but not limited to device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, etc. This information is used to optimize the App's performance and improve user experience.

1.4 Location Information

To provide navigation functionality, the App may use your device's location information. You can choose to grant or deny the App access to your location information. If you choose to deny, it may affect the use of navigation features.
